Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

TL;DR: Your documents stay on your device. We use Anthropic's Claude AI to process them, but we don't store, share, or train on your data.

Overview

Papertrail Copilot ("we," "our," or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

1. Documents & Photos

  • Documents you scan are processed by AI to extract tasks and information
  • Photos are stored locally on your device only
  • Documents are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI API for processing
  • We do not store your documents on our servers

2. Task Data

  • Tasks, due dates, and descriptions you create
  • Stored locally on your device
  • Not transmitted to our servers

3. Usage Data

  • Number of AI actions used per month (for free tier limits)
  • App feature usage for improving the app
  • Anonymous crash reports and diagnostics

4. AI Processing

  • When you scan documents or generate drafts, content is sent to Anthropic Claude AI API
  • Anthropic's privacy policy applies: anthropic.com/legal/privacy
  • According to Anthropic's policy, they do not train AI models on user data

What We Don't Collect

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Contact list access
  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • ❌ We do not use your documents for any purpose beyond providing the service

How We Use Your Information

  • Process documents with AI to extract tasks and deadlines
  • Provide reminders and notifications for your tasks
  • Track usage limits for free tier (10 actions per month)
  • Improve app functionality and fix bugs

Data Storage

  • All documents and tasks are stored locally on your device
  • Optional iCloud backup (controlled by your iOS settings)
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • Data remains on your device until you delete the app

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude AI: For document processing and AI-powered features
  • Apple Push Notification Service: For task reminders (iOS system service)

Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Disable AI features and use the app offline
  • Turn off notifications in iOS Settings
  • Control iCloud backup in iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • All data stored locally on your device is protected by iOS security
  • Transmission to Anthropic AI is encrypted (HTTPS)
  • No permanent storage of your documents on external servers

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

International Users

If you are located outside the United States, please note that your information will be processed through Anthropic's services, which may involve transfer to and processing in different countries.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or shared
  • Access their personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information

Note: We do not sell personal information. All your data is stored locally on your device.

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are in the European Union:

  • Legal basis for processing: Consent and contract performance
  • You have the right to access, rectify, erase, and port your data
  • You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting the app
